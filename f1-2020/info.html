Within a League, a group of players (supplemented with AI-drivers) battle in a virtual Formula One season in numerous Grand Prixs to become the fastest overall. Over the course of the season all GP-results are kept up, and a driver and team ranking is made. Points are distributed as they would in an actual Formula One Grand Prix. Our league is currently in it's first season. We play under the following rules:

Rule Setting
AI Driver Level 40%
AI Reserve Driver Level 30%
Collisions Enabled - Full Vehicle Damage
Ghosting Disabled
Corner Cutting Stringency Strict
Breaking Assist Anti-Lock Brakes Enabled
ERS & DRS Assist Disabled
Fuel Assist Disabled

In an ideal scenario, there will be one race every twpo weeks, on Saturday. A 30 minutes Free Practice session starts at 16:00, after which there is an 18 minutes Qualifying session, followed by the race. The formation lap should go ahead at last at 17:10 (Brussles Time).
